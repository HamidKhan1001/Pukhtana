Tribal Organizational Chart

From the legendary ancestor Qais Abdur Rashid — every branch of the Pashtoon nation

According to Pashtoon genealogical tradition (Shajra), all Pashtoons descend from Qais Abdur Rashid — who met the Prophet's companion Khalid ibn Walid around 630 CE and returned to his people to spread Islam. His three sons — Sarban, Bitan (Betan), and Ghurghusht — became the ancestors of all the major tribal confederacies. A fourth branch, Karlani, has disputed origins but is fully Pashtoon.
